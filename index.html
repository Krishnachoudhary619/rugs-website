Our Story

Rooted in craft.
Built for the world.

Zafran House was founded on a single conviction: that the finest Indian rugs — woven in workshops passed down through generations — deserve a global audience that truly understands their worth.

We work directly with weaving communities across Jaipur, Mirzapur, Bhadohi, and Kashmir. Every rug in our catalogue is sourced firsthand, quality-checked under natural light, and documented with full provenance — the region, the weave technique, the dye source.

For importers, interior designers, and boutique retailers seeking something genuine — not factory-produced, not algorithmically curated — Zafran House is where the search ends.

The making

Craft you can
trace to its source

We believe buyers deserve to know exactly how a rug was made. Each piece in our export catalogue comes with full documentation — from fibre origin to finishing method.

i.
Sourcing from weavers
We visit workshops directly, working with families who have practised their craft for three or four generations. No middlemen, no wholesale markets.
ii.
Natural dyeing
Most of our collection uses vegetable dyes — pomegranate rind, indigo, madder root, turmeric. Colours that deepen, not fade, over decades.
iii.
Hand-finishing & QC
Every rug is inspected under natural light for knot density, colour fastness, pile evenness, and dimensional accuracy before it is cleared for export.
iv.
Export documentation
We handle GST invoicing, DGFT export licensing, and all customs paperwork. Sea or air freight, door-to-port or CIF — your preference.
